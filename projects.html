PromptLab

"The Postman of LLM Propmts."

getpromptlab.io

PromptLab is a management platform for Large Language Model prompts. Our users are teams building software on top of LLMs, and the benefit of using our platform is the ability to collaboratively store, test, and optimize prompts.

Current Features:

  • Prompt version control
  • Automatic zero-shot-prompt optimization
  • Collaboration features
  • Separate

PromptLab is currently free to test on our site, and is being actively developed. We plan on adding in support for vector databases, allowing users to test their prompts over the same data that they would use in a production environment.
